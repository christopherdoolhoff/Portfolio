About Me

Christopher Doolhoff is the National Transportation Manager at Thatcher Transportation, responsible for Logistics Management, Carrier Negotiation, and Strategic Planning. He has played a key role in facilitating growth in California and Florida markets as well as improved performance in the New York operations. In 2009, Christopher graduated from Weber State University with a bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management. He learned full stack web development through the UCF coding boot camp in 2022-2023. He lives in Deland, FL with his wife and kids.
